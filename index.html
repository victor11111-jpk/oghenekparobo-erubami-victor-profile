Biography

Full Name: Oghenekparobo Erubami Victor

Date of Birth: September 21, 2002

Place of Birth: Benin City, Edo, Nigeria

Nationality: Nigerian

State of Origin: Delta State, Nigeria

Oghenekparobo Erubami Victor is a Nigerian public figure recognized for his influence in politics, lifestyle, relationships, entertainment, and technology discussions. He completed his primary education in Delta State before moving to Benin City, Edo State, where he attended Benin Technical College and later Ezomo College, writing his WAEC examinations. He also studied Welding and Fabrication in Benin, acquiring technical skills in structural fabrication, metalwork, and precision welding.

Beyond technical skills, Victor is a creative personality with talents in communication, media, and innovation. He blends craftsmanship with public engagement, using his platform to inspire, educate, and connect with audiences across various topics. His versatility makes him a distinctive personality in both professional and social spaces.
